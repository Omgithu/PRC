About MagicLights

MagicLights is a prototype of a voice-controlled home automation system. It is an IoT based project which was developed using Arduino, a bluetooth module and a relay module. It uses the user's voice commands to switch on/off lights.

  • Category: IoT

About Chuchumbu Bot

A funny, talking robot developed using a voice module, servo motors, IR sensor module and Arduino. Whenever you try to bring your hand close to its cheeks, it will try to remove your hand with its own automated hand and will say its name aloud. This is the first robot of the “Bum Bhole” series of robots and was developed to present it to a friend on his birthday. Currently working on how to incorporate machine learning into this robot.
